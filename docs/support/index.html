Pastura Support

Pastura is an iOS app for running AI multi-agent simulations fully on-device. This page is the support and feedback channel for the app.

Contact the maintainer

Use the contact form below to send feedback, bug reports, or questions. For general feedback, leave the Scenario ID field blank.

Open the contact form

You will receive an automatic acknowledgement at the email address you provide on the form. The maintainer reviews submissions and replies as needed.

Prefer a public tracker?

You can also file a GitHub issue on the project repository. This requires a GitHub account and is public.

Reporting a Share Board scenario

To report a specific scenario shown in the in-app Share Board, use the Report button on the scenario's detail sheet inside the app. The in-app flow pre-fills the scenario identifier automatically; the destination is the same contact form above.

Reports indicating clear policy violations are hidden from the gallery during triage. Triage is performed by the Pastura maintainer. The full report-handling policy is documented in ADR-005 §6.
